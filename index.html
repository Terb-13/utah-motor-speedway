Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book track time, karting, or Rocket Rally?
Use the “Book Now” buttons or contact us directly. We’ll walk you through availability and options.
What’s the largest group you can accommodate?
Up to 14,000 in the grandstands for major events, or private groups from 10–300 in our meeting and hospitality spaces.
Do you host corporate events and team building?
Absolutely. We specialize in creating custom racing experiences for companies and groups of any size — including our new Rocket Rally Experience.
What track configurations are available?
We offer four main layouts: 4.448-mile Full Course, 3.048-mile Outer Course, and two 2.2-mile East/West courses — plus dedicated karting, off-road, rock crawling, and rallycross areas.
When will the Private Garages be available?
We’re currently building the vision and accepting early interest. Join the waitlist to be the first to know when applications open.
Is the facility suitable for large public events like concerts or festivals?
Yes. We have the infrastructure, seating, media capabilities, and on-site support to host world-class races, concerts, festivals, car shows, and more.
