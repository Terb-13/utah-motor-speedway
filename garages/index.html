Private garages for supercars,
race cars, and the life above them

A restrained collection of bays designed for machines that deserve silence, climate, and a straight sightline to the circuit — with curated living and office space above, so the club is never just where you store the car. It is where you plan the next season.

Built for the intersection of collection and competition

Whether your stable centers on road-going supercars or dedicated race hardware, the vision is the same: a private, climate-controlled sanctuary steps from the track — and a second level reserved for how you actually live and lead when you are not in the seat.

  • Access

    Direct track access

    From climate-controlled bay to circuit entry — no trailers, no compromise. Your cars wake up where they were meant to run.

  • Environment

    Climate-controlled precision

    Engineered for high-value machinery and the details collectors notice — stable air, refined finishes, and discretion by design.

  • Exclusivity

    Limited availability for founding members

    This is not a mass offering. Founding memberships will be intentionally capped — priority for those who intend to build a long-term relationship with the club.

When applications open, founding members will be contacted in order of interest. One step secures your place in line.
